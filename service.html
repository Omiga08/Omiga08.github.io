MRI Machine

Use powerful magnets and radio waves to create detailed images of internal body structures.

X-ray Machine

Capture images of bones and tissues, commonly used to diagnose fractures and infections.

Surgical Operation

Ultrasound Machine

An ultrasound, also known as sonography, is a noninvasive imaging technique used in hospitals to create real-time images or videos of the inside of the body.

CT Scanner

Computed Tomography (CT) scanners are advanced medical imaging devices that Provide cross-sectional images of the body, useful for detecting tumors, injuries, and abnormalities.

ECG Machine

sophisticated medical device used to measure and record the electrical activity of the heart over a period of time.used to diagnose various heart conditions, such as arrhythmias.
